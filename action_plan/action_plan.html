Slide 1/15

Business Problem

  • Manual RFQ handling is slow and inefficient
  • Agent emails are unstructured and inconsistent
  • Rate comparison is error-prone and time-consuming
  • Carrier DO charges vary significantly
  • ETD decisions directly impact customer trust
  • Pricing managers are overloaded with routine tasks
Slide 2/15

System Objective

  • Fully automate FCL pricing from China to UAE
  • AI reads and processes customer RFQs automatically
  • AI validates enquiry completeness in real-time
  • System sends RFQs to multiple China agents simultaneously
  • AI analyzes rates with carrier performance history
  • Manager selects best agent with AI recommendations
  • System calculates final door-to-door price
  • System quotes customer instantly with standardized format
Slide 3/15

Customer Enquiry Processing

  • Customer sends RFQ by email to dedicated system address
  • System reads email via IMAP with automatic parsing
  • AI extracts critical information:
    • POL (Port of Loading), POD (Port of Discharge)
    • Incoterm requirements
    • Container type & quantity
    • Cargo type and special requirements
    • Cargo readiness date and time
  • If any information missing → AI replies on same thread requesting clarification
Slide 4/15

Agent RFQ Automation

  • Validated RFQ sent to 10 fixed China agents simultaneously
  • Always address agent by name for personalized communication
  • Delivery location automatically converted to Jebel Ali Port
  • All RFQs sent in USD for consistency
  • Standardized RFQ format to all agents for easy comparison
  • System tracks delivery and read receipts
Slide 5/15

China Time Logic

  • System automatically converts enquiry time to China time (UTC+8)
  • Business Hours (09:00–17:00):
    • Wait 4 hours for agent replies
    • Send automated reminder at 3 hours if <4 quotes received
  • After Hours (After 17:00):
    • Wait until next business day 11:00
    • Send automated reminder at 10:00
Slide 6/15

Agent Rate Analysis

  • AI reads and parses all agent replies automatically
  • Extracts key information:
    • Ocean Freight (main cost component)
    • Carrier name and service level
    • ETD (Estimated Time of Departure)
    • Transit Time to destination
    • Free time at destination port
    • Additional charges and validity period
  • AI ranks best options based on multiple factors
  • Considers carrier DO charge impact on final price
Slide 7/15

Pricing Manager Control

  • Pricing manager has final approval and override authority
  • Manager reviews AI recommendations on dashboard
  • Manager selects which agent to use based on experience
  • System locks selected rate for calculation
  • All further steps are completely automatic
  • Audit trail maintained for all decisions
Slide 8/15

Cost Calculation Engine

  • Ocean Freight (in USD from agent)
  • Currency Conversion USD → AED @ 3.685
  • UAE Destination Charges:
    • DO charges = document fee + per container charge
    • All UAE charges except BOE & DPC are per container
  • Transport to warehouse (distance-based)
  • Customs clearance and handling fees
Slide 9/15

Margin & Rounding Rules

  • Add 13% profit margin to total cost
  • Always round to nearest 10 AED
  • No decimals in final quotation
  • No values ending in .01, .05 etc. - clean pricing only
  • Margin breakdown visible to management only
  • Competitive pricing algorithm for market adjustment
Slide 10/15

Customer Quotation

  • Reply only on original RFQ thread
  • No new email loop - maintains conversation history
  • Standard quotation format with company branding
  • Complete transparency:
    • Carrier name and service details
    • ETD and Transit Time clearly shown
    • All exclusions and special conditions
    • Quotation validity period
  • Automated follow-up after 24 hours if no response
Slide 11/15

AI Learning Engine

  • Tracks which agent wins most by specific route
  • Monitors carrier complaint and performance history
  • Improves ranking automatically based on success rate
  • Optimizes future RFQ waiting logic and timing
  • Learns from manager overrides for better suggestions
  • Predictive analytics for rate fluctuations
